## Node 22 – Karma (Conservation Ledger)

### Summary

Karma isn’t cosmic punishment; it’s a balancing ledger.  Every action produces a reaction, conserving energy and information【866079294454206†L534-L548】.

### Science

The **law of conservation of energy** states that energy cannot be created or destroyed, only transformed.  Similarly, **conservation of information** (unitarity) ensures that quantum processes are reversible.  Karma functions like a ledger that records inputs and outputs.  In thermodynamics, entropy change reflects the cost of irreversibility.  In causal inference, actions have consequences that propagate through networks.  Ancient texts treat karma as the mechanism by which the universe maintains equilibrium.

### Encoding (Generative Art)

* **Transaction logs:** Visualise actions as entries in a ledger.  Each user interaction (click, sound) adds a line that must be balanced by an opposite action.
* **Reactive environment:** Build a scene where every interaction has an equal and opposite effect somewhere else.  For example, creating a shape removes mass or colour from another region.
* **Ledger balancing game:** Gamify equilibrium by asking the viewer to maintain balance among resources.  Violations cause glitches or noise to build up until corrected.

### Underlayer (System Hack)

You can’t cheat physics.  Everything you do echoes.  Rather than fearing karma, use it as **feedback**: what you put out is what you get back.  In code, remember that technical debt accumulates.  Clean up after yourself.  In life, treat your actions as entries; maintain your ledger and watch the universe sync.
