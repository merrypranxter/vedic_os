## Node 17 – Hiranyagarbha (Golden Cosmic Egg)

### Summary

Before the big bang, there was the **Hiraṇyagarbha**—the golden womb or cosmic egg.  It contains all potential universes, poised to crack open【866079294454206†L411-L423】.

### Science

Cosmology posits a **primordial singularity** preceding the expansion.  The cosmic microwave background is the afterglow of this event.  The term *cosmic egg* appears in many cultures (Orphic egg, Brahmāṇḍa).  It represents a dense, hot, homogeneous state.  As it “hatches,” spacetime and matter emerge.  Inflationary models describe an exponential expansion following the initial singularity.  The golden hue symbolises high energy density and symmetry.

### Encoding (Generative Art)

* **Egg explosion:** Model a golden sphere that cracks and explodes into particles.  Use fluid simulation or particle emitters to depict the big bang.
* **Glowing embryo:** Place a luminous embryo at the centre of a dark void.  Slowly increase its brightness until it bursts.
* **Nested potentials:** Draw concentric shells with increasing complexity.  The viewer can peel away shells to reveal new universes inside.

### Underlayer (System Hack)

All potential is compressed into your consciousness before you act.  The golden egg invites you to recognise the **pause before creation** as a potent state.  In coding, honour the time spent planning before you execute; in life, realise your world emerges from your inner stillness.  Use golden eggs as visual metaphors for projects incubating in your mind.
