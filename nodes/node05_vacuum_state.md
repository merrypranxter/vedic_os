## Node 5 – Vacuum State (Śūnya & Zero‑Point)

### Summary

Emptiness isn’t nothing; it’s a seething potential.  Vedic texts refer to **Śūnya**—the void—as pregnant with creation, akin to the quantum vacuum【866079294454206†L90-L105】.

### Science

In quantum field theory, the “vacuum” is not truly empty; it is the lowest energy state of a field, bubbling with virtual particles and fluctuations.  The **Higgs field** pervades space, giving particles mass.  **Zero‑point energy** has observable effects such as the Casimir force.  Vedic philosophy similarly describes the void (śūnya) as the womb of all things, a fertile emptiness where forms emerge and dissolve.
