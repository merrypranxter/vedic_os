Modern physics describes a similar idea in **Conformal Cyclic Cosmology**. The entropy of black holes and cosmic microwave background radiation hints at previous “aeons.”  In Vedic terms, Kali Yuga marks maximum entropy; the cosmic ledger is wiped clean and a new Satya Yuga emerges from the ashes.
