## Node 19 – Prāṇa (Zero‑Point Life Force)

### Summary

Prāṇa is the subtle life force animating all beings.  It corresponds to the **dark energy** or vacuum energy that drives cosmic expansion【866079294454206†L442-L454】.

### Science

Observations of Type Ia supernovae and the cosmic microwave background indicate that the expansion of the universe is accelerating.  This implies the presence of **dark energy**, often modelled as vacuum energy with negative pressure.  The Vedas describe prāṇa as pervasive, moving through nāḍīs and powering the body.  Breathing exercises (prāṇāyāma) alter prāṇa flow.  In physics, zero‑point energy permeates the vacuum and may manifest as the cosmological constant.  Both concepts point to an all‑pervading energy field.
