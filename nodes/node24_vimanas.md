## Node 24 – Vimānas (Mercury Vortex Engines)

### Summary

The ancient **Vaimānika Śāstra** describes flying machines powered by mercury vortex engines.  These **Vimānas** prefigure concepts in magnetohydrodynamic (MHD) propulsion【866079294454206†L560-L574】.

### Science

MHD propulsion uses magnetic and electric fields to accelerate a conducting fluid without moving parts.  Mercury has high electrical conductivity and density, making it a plausible working fluid.  Experiments have demonstrated small MHD thrusters using saltwater.  The Vaimānika Śāstra outlines circular machines with mercury spinning at high speed to generate lift and directional control.  Whether literal or symbolic, the concept aligns with modern plasma drives and ion thrusters used in spacecraft.
